Help, Guide & FAQ

Quick-Start Guide
1. Create a Booking
When you receive a booking by phone, WhatsApp, or email, immediately add it in the system. Check vehicle availability on the pickup date, select a vehicle and driver, and confirm the booking. This prevents double-booking and missed trips.
→ Add Booking
2. Trip Planning
After confirming a booking: check vehicle availability (shown on dashboard), assign the vehicle, call the driver, and use the system to send booking details. The driver will be notified for trips starting tomorrow.
→ View Dashboard
3. Add Duty Slip
After every trip, the driver submits a duty slip (or you enter it on their behalf). Enter pickup/drop times, odometer readings, and locations. This creates the billing record. Missing slips are highlighted in red on the dashboard.
→ Add Duty Slip
4. Generate Invoice
Select a company, choose the billing period and trips with submitted slips. The system auto-calculates charges based on the company's rate card (base rate, extra KM, extra hours, tolls). Preview and generate the invoice.
→ Generate Invoice
5. Send & Track Bills
Each company has a preferred billing mode (Email, WhatsApp, Post, GeM Portal, Vendor Site). Send the invoice accordingly and mark it as "Sent". Set a payment due date to get follow-up reminders.
→ All Invoices
6. Record Payment
When a company pays, go to All Payments and click "Mark Paid". Enter the payment date, amount, mode (NEFT, Cheque, UPI, Cash), and UTR/reference number. Overdue invoices appear as alerts on the dashboard.
→ All Payments
Business Process Flow
  • Booking Received (Call / Email / WhatsApp)
    Immediately enter in TravelBook → Add Booking. Check vehicle availability on that date. Assign vehicle and driver. Confirm the booking. Status = Confirmed.
  • Trip Planning (Day before)
    Review tomorrow's trips on the Dashboard. Call driver to confirm. Send driver details to client. The system highlights upcoming trips for next day.
  • Trip in Progress
    Dashboard shows "Ongoing" trips. Any issues → update booking status. Trip Start = record start odometer. Trip End = record end odometer.
  • Duty Slip Collection (After Trip)
    Driver submits duty slip (or gives it to owner). Enter in system: Add Duty Slip. Link to the booking. System alerts if slip not submitted within 24 hours of trip completion.
  • Invoice Generation (Weekly/Monthly)
    Go to Generate Invoice. Select company + period. System lists all unbilled trips with submitted duty slips. Select trips, verify amounts (auto-calculated from rate card), and generate invoice. Review + print/PDF.
  • Bill Submission to Client
    Based on company's registered billing mode send the bill. Mark invoice as "Sent". System tracks due date for payment.
  • Payment Follow-up
    Dashboard alerts for overdue invoices. Follow up with company contact. Mark reminders sent. Escalate if overdue >30 days.
  • Payment Received
    Mark invoice as Paid. Enter payment date, amount, mode (NEFT/Cheque/UPI/Cash), and reference number. Reports update automatically.
Frequently Asked Questions
How do I check which vehicles are available on a date? ▼
On the Dashboard (Home page), there is a Fleet Availability table at the bottom showing real-time status of all vehicles. When adding a booking, the system automatically shows available vehicles for the selected pickup date using color-coded chips (green = available, grey = booked).
What are the booking types and their inclusions? ▼
  • Full Day: 300 km / 10 hours included. Extra KM and extra hours billed at company rate.
  • Local: 80 km / 8 hours included.
  • Airport Pickup / Drop: 30 km / 3 hours included.
  • Outstation: Per km rate applies. Night halt charges extra.
Rates differ per company. Check the rate card shown when generating an invoice.
How does the Missing Duty Slip alert work? ▼
In All Duty Slips, bookings with "Completed" status that do not have a corresponding duty slip are highlighted in red and shown with a "Missing" badge. The Dashboard also shows a count of missing slips. Enter the duty slip as soon as you receive it from the driver to clear this alert.
How are invoice amounts calculated automatically? ▼
When generating an invoice, TravelBook reads the company's rate card (base rate for trip type, extra KM rate, extra hour rate, night halt rate). It compares the actual KM/hours from the duty slip against the included limits and calculates extra charges. Toll charges from the duty slip are added separately. GST is applied as set in the rate card (usually 5%).
How do I handle different billing modes per company? ▼
Each company has a registered preferred billing mode (shown in the "Bill Mode" column in All Invoices). Common modes in use:
  • Email: Download PDF and email to company accounts.
  • WhatsApp: Send PDF via WhatsApp to the company contact.
  • GeM Portal: Log in to GeM (gem.gov.in) and submit invoice there.
  • Vendor Site: Log in to the company's vendor portal and upload.
What data do I need for GST compliance? ▼
TravelBook tracks GST collected per invoice (CGST 2.5% + SGST 2.5% = 5% for transportation). For your monthly GST return (GSTR-1), use the Sales Report filtered for the month to get: B2B invoice-wise data, GST amounts, company GSTINs. For input tax credit, check the Expenses Report for bills with GST from vendors.
How do I record and categorise business expenses for income tax? ▼
Go to Reports → Expenses Details and click "+ Add Expense". Categorize expenses under: Fuel, Driver Salary, Maintenance, Insurance, Toll/Parking, Office/Admin. Keep the receipt/bill and upload it. At year-end, the Expenses Report gives you a category-wise summary required for your income tax filing (business expenses under Chapter VI-A).
