❓ Frequently Asked Questions
How do I submit a GeM invoice on HPCL VSS portal? ▼
  1. Login to the VSS portal using your Vendor Code and password.
  2. Navigate to Invoices → GeM Invoice Submission.
  3. Enter your Purchase Order number in the search box and press Enter.
  4. Select the GeM Invoice from the dropdown (GEM-XXXXXXXX).
  5. Fill in the HPCL Location/Payment Details (Receiving Plant, Consignee, Bank Account).
  6. Select the Type of Invoice and upload the signed invoice PDF.
  7. Click Submit Invoice. You will receive a confirmation number.
Why is my invoice showing "Pending" status for a long time? ▼
Invoice processing typically takes 5–10 working days. Status may remain "Pending" due to:
  • Missing or incorrect supporting documents
  • KYV verification incomplete (check My Profile)
  • Bank account details not verified
  • Discrepancy in GSTIN or invoice amount vs. GeM portal data
  • Approval pending from the consignee / engineer-in-charge
Contact the HPCL Accounts Payable team at accounts.payable@hpcl.in if it has been more than 10 working days.
How do I complete my KYV (Know Your Vendor) verification? ▼
  1. Go to My Profile → KYV Documents tab.
  2. Upload each required document (PAN Card, GST Certificate, Cancelled Cheque, Trade License).
  3. Documents should be in PDF or clear image format. Max size: 2 MB per file.
  4. Click Submit KYV Documents after uploading all documents.
  5. KYV verification is typically completed within 3 working days.
  6. You will receive an email notification once verified.
How do I download my TDS certificate (Form 16A)? ▼

TDS certificates are generated quarterly after TDS return filing by HPCL.

  1. Navigate to TDS → TDS Certificates.
  2. Select the Financial Year and Quarter from the filters.
  3. Click Search to view available certificates.
  4. Click the Download button next to the relevant certificate.

Certificates are usually available by the 15th of the month following the quarter end. If unavailable, check the TRACES portal directly.

What is the e-Measurement Book (MB) and when do I need to submit it? ▼
The e-Measurement Book (MB) is a digital record of the quantum of work or services measured at site. It is required for:
  • Works contracts (civil, electrical, mechanical services)
  • Monthly running account (RA) bills
  • Any PO where payment is linked to measured quantities

MB entries must be submitted within 7 days of the measurement date. The Engineer-in-Charge at HPCL must counter-sign the MB before invoicing.

Can I raise a refund request? What are the conditions? ▼
Refund requests can be raised under the following conditions:
  • Excess payment received compared to invoice value
  • Duplicate payment by HPCL
  • Services or goods not rendered / rejected
  • Price discrepancy between PO and invoice

Go to Refund → Raise Refund Request. Select the relevant invoice, enter the refund amount and reason, upload supporting documents, and submit. Refund processing typically takes 15–20 working days.

How do I update or add a bank account for payment? ▼
  1. Go to My Profile → Bank Accounts tab.
  2. Click + Add Bank Account to add a new account.
  3. Fill in the Account No., IFSC code, and branch details.
  4. Upload a cancelled cheque (clear scan, PDF or image format).
  5. Click Update Bank Details.
  6. The new account will be verified by HPCL Finance within 2–3 working days before it can be used for payments.
I forgot my password. How do I reset it? ▼
  1. On the login page, click Forgot Password.
  2. Enter your Vendor Code (SAP Vendor No.) and registered email address.
  3. You will receive a one-time password (OTP) on your registered email/mobile.
  4. Use the OTP to reset your password.
  5. Alternatively, contact the HPCL Vendor Helpdesk at 1800-22-4722.
